How Prop Radar works

Modeled after the PropFirmMatch funnel: discovery → comparison → click-to-buy → loyalty points → reviews & repeat usage.

  1. Search & compare. Traders land on firm lists or niche pages (e.g. “Best futures firms”), filter by assets, platforms and rules, then open firm detail pages.
  2. Choose a challenge. They compare evaluation plans, reading about profit targets, drawdown, scaling, payout rules and user reviews.
  3. Click “Buy”. The marketplace attaches tracking parameters and sends the trader to the firm’s checkout page.
  4. Confirm purchase & earn points. After checkout, traders upload confirmation screenshots or IDs. Once verified, loyalty points are credited.
  5. Leave reviews & come back. Traders share their experiences, mark favorite firms and use points to try other challenges.

Behind the scenes (PM lens)

  • Search & filter service indexing firms and challenges.
  • Affiliate tracking & reconciliation with partner reports.
  • Loyalty ledger per user, with a points → rewards mapping.
  • Review verification and moderation pipeline.
  • Analytics & dashboards for both internal PMs and prop-firm partners.
