Loyalty points & rewards

Balance: 0 pts

Modeled after PropFirmMatch: traders earn points for every challenge plan purchased through the marketplace, which can later be redeemed for free plans or discounts.

  1. Find a firm and challenge via the comparison tools.
  2. Click Buy to go to the prop firm’s checkout with a tracked link.
  3. After purchase, upload your order confirmation within the allowed window.
  4. Once verified, points are credited to your Prop Radar loyalty account.
